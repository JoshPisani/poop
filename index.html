POOP HEAD IS A MULTIPLAYER CARD GAME

THE OBJECT OF THE GAME IS TO FINISH THE DECK AND GET RID OF ALL YOUR CARDS

THE LAST PLAYER WITH CARDS REMAINING IS THE POOP HEAD

AT THE START OF THE GAME EACH PLAYER IS DEALT 3 CARDS FACE DOWN AND 6 FACE UP

PLAYERS EACH PICK 3 CARDS TO KEEP IN THEIR HAND AND 1 TO PLACE FACE UP ON EACH OF THE FACE DOWN CARDS

ON A PLAYERS TURN THEY MUST PLAY ONE OR MORE CARDS OF THE SAME OR HIGHER VALUE THAN THE PREVIOUSLY PLAYED CARD

ALL CARDS PLAYED IN A TURN MUST BE OF THE SAME VALUE

PLAYING FOUR CARDS OF THE SAME VALUE WILL CLEAR THE PILE AND GIVE YOU ANOTHER TURN

IF THERE ARE NO CARDS IN THE PILE YOU CAN PLAY ANYTHING

IF YOU CANNOT BEAT THE CARD ON THE TOP OF THE PILE YOU MUST PICK UP THE PILE

YOU MAY ALSO PICK UP THE PILE ON ANY TURN

AT THE END OF YOUR TURN IF YOU HAVE LESS THAN 3 CARDS IN YOUR HAND YOU MUST DRAW CARDS UNTIL YOU HAVE 3

WHEN THERE ARE NO CARDS LEFT IN YOUR DECK AND IN YOUR HAND YOU PLAY USING THE FACE UP CARDS YOU SELECTED AT THE START OF THE GAME

WHEN YOU HAVE PLAYED ALL YOUR FACE UP CARDS YOU SELECT FROM THE FACE DOWN CARDS - THESE ARE PLAYED AS SOON AS THEY ARE REVEALED

CERTAIN CARDS HAVE SPECIAL RULES

2 - CAN BE PLAYED AT ANY TIME

7 - TO BEAT A 7, YOU MUST PLAY CARDS OF EQUAL OR LOWER VALUE - (OPTIONAL) CAN BE PLAYED AT ANY TIME - (OPTIONAL) REVERSES PLAYER TURN ORDER

8 - CAN BE PLAYED AT ANY TIME - TO BEAT AN 8, YOU MUST BEAT THE VALUE OF THE CARD BELOW THE 8

10 - CAN BE PLAYED AT ANY TIME - DISCARDS THE PILE - PLAYING A 10 GETS YOU ANOTHER TURN

JOKER - CAN BE PLAYED AT ANY TIME - PICK A PLAYER, THEY PICK UP THE WHOLE PILE AND YOU GET ANOTHER TURN (YOU CAN GIVE THE PILE TO A PLAYER WHO HAS NO CARDS TO GET THEM BACK IN THE GAME)
